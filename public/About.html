About

Welcome to Quotient! This is a website where users can discover quotes to spark their creativity. If you are stuck, perhaps in a writer's block, then this is the perfect site for you. There are a few ways to discover some inspiring words from all sorts of authors. The first way is to get the quote of the day. The next is the get some random quotes via the Discover Quotes section. This will display random quotes that you can look through, like, and save. Finally, you are able to search for quotes based on any keyword you can think of. That is only available via the Home page. You are able to save and like quotes. Simply click the heart to like and it will appear red, then move over to the "Likes" page on the side bar, and viola. To save quotes, it is the same process, click on the star and it will turn to yellow. Then, navigate over to the "Saved" section on the side bar.
